# AngularJS - Modules
Allows us to insert dynamic values into our `HTML` - [Angular Expressions](https://docs.angularjs.org/guide/expression)

## Basic

### Numeric Operations
```xml
<p>
  I am {{ 4 + 6 }}
  <!-- I am 10 -->
</p>
```

### String Operations
```xml
<p>
  {{ "Hello" + " you" }}
  <!-- hello you -->
</p>
```
